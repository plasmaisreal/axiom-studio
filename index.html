How is this different from hiring a regular web designer?

+
A traditional designer takes 4–12 weeks and $3,000–$15,000 for a site that doesn't include any automation. AXIOM uses AI to compress that timeline to days and includes the AI chatbot, CRM, and automation sequences that a regular designer won't build. You get more, faster, for less.

What happens after I buy?

+
Within 24 hours of purchase, you'll receive a 10-minute intake form. Once submitted, our AI engine starts building within 48 hours. You'll receive a review link for feedback, and after approval, we go live. The whole process is fast and guided — no technical knowledge needed from you.

Do I need to provide content or photos?

+
No. Our AI generates compelling copy based on your intake form. We source licensed imagery. If you have brand photos or specific content you want included, even better — but it's not required to get started.

What's the money-back guarantee?

+
If we don't deliver your project within the stated timeframe or you're not satisfied with the quality after your revision round, we'll refund 100% of your purchase. No hassle, no questions. We back our work completely.

Yes. You own 100% of everything we build. Code, content, domain — all yours. We hand off full admin access at launch. No ongoing fees, no lock-in. What we build is permanently yours.

Absolutely. Many clients start with Starter and upgrade to Pro or Elite after seeing results. We apply what you already paid toward the upgrade. Just reach out and we'll handle the transition seamlessly.
